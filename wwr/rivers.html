Rivers

This page is to give information about the rivers that the company runs.

Main Fork of the Salmon River, Idaho

The Salmon is one of the longest undammed rivers in the United States. The river corridor is lined with awe inspiring ponderosa pine trees. There are good rapids all summer, but families with children who like to swim tend to appreciate the late summer's slower, warmer water more.

Main Fork of the Salmon River, Idaho

Desolation Canyon has been recognized as a National Landmark. This 84-mile run runs from from Sand Wash to Swaseys Rapid (just outside Green River Utah) It boasts outstanding scenery, geologic formations, evidence of prehistoric and historic human activity, and great whitewater opportunities.
